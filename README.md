# 💫 About Me:
I’m Bryant, a Frontend Software Engineer from Fort Worth, TX with 3+ years of experience building enterprise‑scale web and mobile experiences in fintech. I specialize in Angular, TypeScript, accessible UI architecture, and high‑quality engineering practices that keep large applications stable, performant, and maintainable.<br><br>I’ve delivered production features used by millions, modernized legacy journeys into scalable mobile components, and led accessibility improvements that strengthened screen‑reader accuracy, semantic structure, and focus behavior across critical workflows. I’m passionate about testing culture and CI/CD readiness—from raising coverage from ~20% to ~80% to surfacing cross‑repo and device‑deployment dependencies early so teams ship with confidence. I’m also known as the teammate who unblocks others, whether it’s accessibility debugging, environment setup, or clarifying developer workflows.<br><br>Outside of engineering, I’m a curious problem‑solver who loves building and experimenting across different mediums. I’m an F1 enthusiast, a photographer, a board game hobbyist, a woodworker, and a 3D‑printing tinkerer who enjoys designing, prototyping, and refining ideas from scratch. I’m always exploring new tools, new skills, and new ways to make things—digital or physical.
